My Favourite Games On Minecraft



Current: Hardcore Mode

One of the main modes in Minecraft is Hardcore mode. In Hardcore mode, you have to eat and fight off monsters like in survival. The catch is you only get one life, and the difficulty is locked on hard. In my world, i've gotten pretty far, having a full iron set of tools and armor. My next goal is to go into the "Nether" and get an essential resource called "Blaze Rods".

All Time: Bedwars

Bedwars is a minigame on the minecraft server, "HYPIXEL". In this minigame, you spawn on 1/9 islands. The aim is to break the other players' bed, as without the bed they don't respawn, meanwhile if they do have a bed, they spawn where the bed is. There is a shop that you can buy items from, and it is bought using a combination of 3 resources: Iron, Diamond, and Emerald. Iron naturally spawns on your island's "forge", meanwhile the other resources spawn on the central island of the game after a set amount of time. Although, diamonds can be used to additionally upgrade the forge to spawn more diamonds/emeralds.
