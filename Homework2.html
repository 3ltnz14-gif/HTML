Liston College

Liston College is a Catholic secondary school located in Auckland, New Zealand.

It was founded in 1974 and is named after Archbishop Michael James Liston. Liston was an important figure in the Cactholic Church of New Zealand.

The school's values include presence, love, courage, and excellence.

Liston College offers a wide range of academic and extracurricular activities, including sports, music, health, and Te Reo Maori, which is the local language of New Zealand.

Liston College has a roll of 835 students and is known for its strong sense of community and commitment to providing a high-quality education.

Liston College is located on 69 Rathgar Road, in Auckland, New Zealand.

For more information, you can visit the school's website at https://www.liston.school.nz/
