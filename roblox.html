My Favourite Games On Roblox



Current: RIVALS

Rivals is a fast-paced 5v5 shooter on Roblox. It can be in first-person or third-person. It featured quick, round-based (1st to 5) combat that circulates around lightning-fast movement, skill-based maps, and especially a wide range of weapons to choose from. There are four categories of weapons: Primary, Secondary, Melee, and Utility. Primary weapons include rifles such as the AR, and long-range weapons such as the Crossbow and Sniper. Secondary weapons include special finishers like the Revolver, or pure damage/skill weapons such as the Energy Pistols which shoot extremely fast but requires really accurate and precise aiming and tracking. Melee weapons each have a special ability, like for Katana, deflecting bullets, or Knife, an instakill backstab. Utility weapons include the Grenade, which can be used for damage or movement, and the Medkit which can be used to heal. It also includes special gamemodes such as FFA where everyone is your enemy or Mirror Matchup where everyone uses the same weapons.

All Time: Fisch

As the name suggests, it's a fishing game. No, the title is not a typo. In this game, you catch fish to get money and buy new rods/boats. There are many different areas you can explore, and over 2,000 differnt fish. Some are fictional, while the rest are mostly found in the ocean, and are actual fish. When you cast your rod, you can either wait, or shake the rod by pressing a ceratin button which makes you get a bite faster. Then you have to play a minigame varying in difficulty involving a line and bar to catch the fish.
