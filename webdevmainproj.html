Roblox

Roblox was one of the first games i've ever played. It encourages creativity, and there is a game for everyone because Roblox is turning into a game engine powered by Lua. Whether it's a FPS shooter game, such as "RIVALS" or "Phantom Forces" or maybe a creative game like "B.A.B.F.T" or "Speed Draw", they've got it all!

Minecraft

Minecraft was the first notable video game I played. I enjoy all 3 modes, Survival, Hardcore, and Creative. In Survival, you have to eat and survive when at night, monsters come and attack you. Hardcore is basically Survival, but you only get one life and the difficulty is set to "Hard". Meanwhile, in Creative, you get infinite resources and invulnerability so you can build anything your heart desires. There are also minigame servers such as Hypixel, where you can play fun minigames such as Bedwars and Skywars.

The Hollow Knight Series

This series contains the games "Hollow Knight", and "Hollow Knight: Silksong." The genre of this series is Metroidvania, aka 2D Platformer. In this series, you play as a vessel in the small kingdoms of Hallownest and Pharloom respectively. You fight off husks possessed by the Infection/Haunting, and aim to cut it off at its' core. I enjoy this game because of the captivating gameplay, and the interesting lore.
