Outline

These Roblox games are some of the most popular games of 2025.

Fisch;
is a Roblox game that was popular throughout 2024, but died, and since then, has made a comeback. It's about, well, fishing! A very relaxing game, except when you get a mythical fish, such as the Colossal Squid or Great White Shark.

Forsaken;
is a horror/survival genre game that appeals to retro Roblox fans, as it takes inspiration from the 2012-2013 era of Roblox.

Steal a Brainrot;
is a fast-paced competitive game where players try to steal a prized "brainrot" item from rivals while avoiding traps and defenders. Steals are short and chaotic, emphasizing stealth, timing, and quick teamwork. Every once in a while, there is an admin abuse, where the developer, SpyderSammy, uses his admin poers to spawn insane brainrots and increase luck.
